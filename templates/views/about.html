Welcome to FilmFinder!

Easily find films and TV shows using the search function which you can then be added to your watchlist for safekeeping!

Every TV show and Film found by this site is courtesy of the Utelly Universal Search which allows for the discovery of entertainment on most major UK and US entertainment streaming services. From this FilmFinder website you can easily store TV shows and films for later viewing and come back to easily access them via the links in your watchlist

Please note that many of the entertainment services that are searched require a paid subscription, however, you may also find free content

Privacy Policy

This FilmFinder web application only collects the bare minimum of information necessary to get you started on your entertainment finding journey. The only personal information stored will include your Username, Email, Secured Password and also the watchlist associated with your account. Please note that all this information will remain secured in our database and will not be shared for commercial or advertisement purposes.

In addition, we also offer an ad-free service! Please enjoy searching and we hope you find your desired content
